# Sourcing Policy Corrected + First Activation Draft

> [!important] Lead directive encoded (docs in rank.ts / keywords.ts)
> 1. Real work + real traction → highest. 2. Real work, no traction yet → high, slightly under. 3. Hype without work → low. Stars are neither a reward nor an exclusion ANYWHERE in sourcing. The pre-seed gate is early-stage-ness, not obscurity.

## Changes

- **Star ceiling removed** from discovery queries (was `stars:3..40`; now `stars:>=2` substance floor only). Early stage enforced via repo age (created ≤9 months, pushed ≤3 weeks).
- Hidden-gem rank bonus capped 15→10 so demonstrated traction (in the composite) beats invisibility at equal capability.
- **Stage gate added to memo decision rules**: companies already past pre-seed/seed → `pass` on stage fit regardless of quality ("strong-but-late founders are tracked, not funded").
- **Outreach is drafts-only, confirmed**: `Outreach.draftMessage` + reason, status DRAFTED; nothing sends anywhere.
